About the Farm

My name is Amy. When my niece, Piper, was little she couldn't say "Amy" and instead called me "Eenie". It was about the same time that I solidified the dream of wanting to own a hobby farm someday. Hence, the beginnings of "Aunt Eenie's Farm" emerged. This is a website/dreamboard for my actual future farm someday. I hope it is a place where family, friends, and people who want to experience the simplicity of honest, hard work come to visit and make memories together. The location is still to be determined. Close enough for family get-togethers would definitley be ideal. So perhaps somewhere in the Pacific Northwest or western Montana.

Chickens, bees, and berries -- oh my!

Organic growth is best for almost anything. So I'll start small with chickens, bees, berries, and a vegetable garden. Then hopefully with time the operation will grow to include a fruit orchard, herb garden, horses, a cow, barn cat, dog, and maybe a pig and goat evetually. All in good time. :) Jaen and Fran will be the names of the miniature horses. Piñata will be the miniature donkey. Sam and Ella will be two of the hens, so you can say you had "Sam & Ella" eggs for breakfast. ;) Mini horse chariot racing will be a must. So swing back by in the future for some fun, fresh air, and dirt under your fingernails.
